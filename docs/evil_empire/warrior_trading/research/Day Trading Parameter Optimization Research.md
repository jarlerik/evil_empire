# Day Trading Parameter Optimization Research

Comprehensive research findings on advanced day trading parameters, compiled April 2026.

## 1. Trailing Stops: Fixed % vs ATR-Based

### Academic Findings
- Zarattini, Aziz, Barbon (2024) — SPY intraday momentum strategy using dynamic trailing stops: 1,985% total return (2007-2024), 19.6% annualized, Sharpe 1.33
- Maroy (2025) — Parameter optimization achieved Sharpe ratios over 3.0 and annualized returns over 50%

### Optimal ATR Multiplier
- Intraday momentum: 1.5-2.0x ATR with 5-10 period ATR lookback
- Starting point: 14-period ATR, 2.0x multiplier

## 2. Optimal Holding Periods
- German blue chip study: 60 minutes optimal holding
- Momentum edges reverse when extended too long
- For 1-min charts: 9 EMA vs 21 EMA crossover as trend filter

## 3. Position Sizing
- Half Kelly: ~75% optimal growth with ~50% less drawdown
- VP + Pyramiding: 20% IRR but 48.69% max drawdown
- RVOL >= 2.0 for entries, breakouts show 40% greater follow-through

## 4. Session Timing
- Best window: 9:50 AM to 10:30 AM
- Most profits in first 45 minutes

## 5. Gap Fills
- 0.1% gaps: ~60% fill rate
- 0.5% gaps: ~42% fill rate
- 1.0% gaps: ~30% fill rate
- Wednesday best day for gap continuation

## 6. Multi-Timeframe
- 15-25% win rate improvement with 3 timeframes
- Optimal combo: 1-min, 5-min, 15-min
